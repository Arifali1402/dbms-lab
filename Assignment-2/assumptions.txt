ASSUMPTIONS ON ER DIAGRAM ON THE APPLICATION MODEL OF SOCCER TEAM:

	1. One Team consists of many Players ( 1-m Relationship).
	2. One Team can have many Coaches ( 1-m Relationship).
	3. One Team can play many Matches ( 1-m Relationship).
	4. Match can be of two different types:
		i. League Matches (league_name,league_pts,league_position).
		i. International Matches (int_name,int_pts,int_position).
